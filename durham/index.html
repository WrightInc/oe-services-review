About Our Durham Operations

Get More Out of Durham Region's Full-Service Utility Contractor

OE Utility Services runs its Durham & Area Regional Business Unit (RBU) from a yard in Courtice, putting crews and equipment close to the heart of one of Ontario's fastest-growing regions. As a full-service utility contractor in Durham Region, OE supports work across Oshawa, Whitby, Ajax, Pickering, and the Municipality of Clarington — from urban tie-ins to large infrastructure programs.

Durham's population is projected to nearly double by 2051, and that growth is driving sustained investment in roads, water and wastewater servicing, gas distribution, and the major energy build underway in Clarington. OE's Courtice base gives crews a short reach into eastern Durham and the surrounding corridor, with the same standard of safety, precision, and accountability OE brings to every project.

As an authorized Enbridge Gas Inc. contractor, OE serves Durham Region with every certification required to operate safely on Ontario's most demanding utility projects — large or small, public or private.
