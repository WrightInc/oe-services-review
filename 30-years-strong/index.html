Greater Toronto Area · Durham & Area · Hamilton & Area · Barrie & Area · London & Area · Ottawa & Area — OE's six Regional Business Units (RBUs) across Ontario.

In 2026, OE Utility Services proudly celebrates 30 years of serving Ontario — and the Customers, crews, and communities who got us here. One truck in a Mississauga lot in 1995. More than 70 trucks across six Regional Business Units (RBUs) in 2026. What began as a single hydrovac and one promise — dig smarter, leave it better — is now an Ontario-wide operation. The promise hasn't changed, and neither has who it's for: our Customers.

The Journey

From One Truck to Six RBUs: 1995 to 2026

  1. 1995

    It starts with one truck.

    Ontario Excavac opens in Mississauga. One hydrovac, one promise: dig smarter, leave it better.

  2. 2008

    Named best in the business.

    Excavator of the Year (Electric), and the move into gas utilities — proof the bet on hydrovac was the right one.

  3. 2015

    Safety becomes the standard.

    The first Utility Contractor Association Safety Award — and every single year since.

  4. 2016

    Room to grow.

    A 95,000 sq. ft., 7-acre GTA home base. The scrappy startup is now an operation.

  5. 2017

    Thinking beyond the dig.

    ClearLine launches; 11,000 water service repairs delivered for the City of Toronto.

  6. 2018

    Closing the loop.

    OE's own soil recycling facility opens — handling what comes out of the ground, responsibly.

  7. 2023

    A new name for a bigger promise.

    Ontario Excavac becomes OE Utility Services — reflecting end-to-end utility support, not just excavation.

  8. 2024

    Across Ontario.

    Ottawa and London open. Hydrovac Excavator of the Year. Recognized as a Safe Employer.

  9. 2025

    Sustainability, verified.

    EcoVadis bronze medal — third-party proof, not just a claim.

  10. 2026

    30 years strong.

    One truck became 70+. One lot became six Regional Business Units. The promise hasn't changed.

The same crews. The same standard. The same promise.Building what Ontario's utilities will rely on for the next thirty years, from our yards across the province to the full range of hydrovac and utility services behind every job. One call, one team, get more out.

Here's to 30 years of progress—and to our next chapter together.
