About Our GTA Operations

Get More Out of the GTA's Most Trusted Utility Contractor

For three decades, OE Utility Services has been the contractor of choice for utility and civil construction across the Greater Toronto Area. Our GTA Regional Business Unit (RBU) is the operational heart of our business — supporting work across Toronto, Mississauga, Brampton, Markham, Vaughan, Richmond Hill, Oakville, Burlington, Pickering, and Ajax.

From emergency gas distribution repairs in downtown Toronto to large-scale subsurface utility engineering for new developments in Vaughan, our GTA crews bring the same standard of safety, precision, and accountability to every project — large or small, public or private.

As an authorized Enbridge Gas Inc. contractor, we serve the GTA with the same standard of safety, precision, and accountability on every project — and hold every certification required to operate safely on Ontario's most demanding utility projects.
